• Whisk eggs in a bowl; add milk and season with salt and white pepper. Whisk until egg mixture is foamy, 2 to 3 minutes.
  • Melt butter in a small, nonstick skillet over medium-low heat.
  • Pour in egg mixture and swirl the skillet, so the bottom is evenly covered with egg.
  • Cook until egg starts to set, about 1 minute. Lift edges with a spatula and tilt the skillet so uncooked egg can flow towards the bottom of the skillet to set.
  • Repeat until no visible liquid egg remains.
  • Carefully flip omelette; cook until warmed through, 30 seconds to 1 minute.
  • Sprinkle cheddar cheese down the middle of omelette; fold in half.
  • Cook until cheese is melted, about 20 seconds.
  • Slide omelette onto a plate.
