• Mix together turkey, brown sugar, salt, black pepper, sage, thyme, marjoram, and red pepper flakes in a bowl until well combined.
  • Shape turkey mixture into patties.
  • Working in batches, fry patties in a large skillet over medium-high heat until golden brown and no longer pink in the center, 6 to 8 minutes.
  • An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
